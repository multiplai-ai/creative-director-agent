# Discovery Intake

This file captures the foundational business context for a brand: what the company does, who it serves, how it makes money, competitive landscape, and customer language.

## What to Include

- Business model and value proposition
- Target market and customer segments
- Competitive set (direct and indirect)
- Customer language and pain points
- Current brand perception
